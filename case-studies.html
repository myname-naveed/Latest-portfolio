🏥 Healthcare

Sahara West Urgent Care

On‑Page + Off‑Page SEO Campaign
Challenge

The clinic had low visibility for urgent care keywords and struggled to compete with larger hospitals.

Strategy
  • Optimized meta titles, descriptions, and heading structure
  • Built high-quality backlinks via citations, directories, and guest posts
  • Implemented schema markup for local healthcare
Implementation
  • 30+ pages optimized with targeted keywords
  • 15+ high-authority backlinks from healthcare directories
  • 5 guest posts on relevant medical blogs
📈 Results: Top 10 rankings for 8 target keywords · 45% increase in organic traffic · Consistent GSC growth
